About Us
We preserve the past to share with you today.

The Junk Shop has been a staple in the Bartlesville community for over 5 years. Our knowledgeable and friendly staff is dedicated to helping you find the perfect piece to add to your collection or to give as a gift. We take great pride in our store and the wide selection of items we offer.

Our inventory is constantly changing and we are always on the lookout for new and interesting items to add to our collection. We specialize in vintage and antique furniture, but also carry a wide variety of other items such as vintage clothing, home decor, and collectibles.We understand the importance of preserving the past, and we do our best to ensure that each piece in our store is in good condition and ready for a new home.

Our team of experts carefully examines each item before it is put up for sale to ensure its authenticity and quality.In addition to selling unique and one-of-a-kind items, we also offer Uhaul rentals to make it easy for you to take your new treasures home. We understand that some items may be too big or fragile to transport on your own, and we want to make the process as easy and stress-free as possible.

We welcome you to visit us at The Junk Shop and explore our vast selection of treasures. Whether you're a collector, a vintage enthusiast, or just looking for a great deal, we have something for everyone. Our store is open nearly every day, so feel free to stop by at any time. We look forward to helping you find the perfect piece to add to your collection or to give as a gift.
